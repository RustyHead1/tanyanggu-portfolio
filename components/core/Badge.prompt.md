A small uppercase status/category marker — softer than Tag, with no hairline capsule.

```jsx
<Badge tone="soft">VR</Badge>
<Badge tone="accent">Now Showing</Badge>
<Badge tone="line">Archive</Badge>
```

Tones: `soft` (dark chip, default) / `line` (hairline) / `accent` (blue fill — use rarely) / `invert` (light fill, black text).
